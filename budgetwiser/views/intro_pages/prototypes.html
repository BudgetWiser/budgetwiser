• Factful

    Factful is a web-based platform to promote public participation in policy making.
    The main goal is to build a Web platform for the public to actively engage
    in policy making, which will provide a new collaborative communication environment.

  • Budgetmap

    Budgetmap polishes budget data into more dynamic and interactive forms
    through visualization methods.
    The main goal of BudgetMap is to give evaluation guidelines to the budget proposals.
